SHOPPING RULES

DESIGNS
  • MAKE READING A HABIT
  • Some of our products have already mock ups/ designs posted on our facebook page that is available to be used as layout for printing
  • If the customer wants to customize their desired design, we will ask additional payment for it.
  • Before we confirm the customer's order, we will ask for the details and the original copy of the design via email to provide the best quality of our products and to avoid complaints.
  • If the design is already agreed upon, it will be considered final and will be printed. Reminder: For additional design or changes with design beyond the agreement, additional charges will incur.
BULK ORDERS
  • We offer a 10% discount for every bulk order made. 20 pcs and above will be considered as bulk order and it must be the same products or design.
  • We require a 50 percent (50%) Down Payment for every bulk order that will be made, the rest will be paid after the delivery of the products for fast and safe transactions.
  • If the number of items for the bulk order has already been agreed. Additional orders will be counted as regular pricing unless the quantity reached 20pcs and above.
CONFIRMED ORDER
  • STRICTLY NO CANCELLATION OF CONFIRMED ORDERS.
  • Strictly no return, no exchange policies (unless seller's fault)
  • If the design is already agreed upon, it will take 2-3 working days to produce the products even if the items/materials are available.
  • If our items/ materials are unavailable, expect delays until our supplies arrived.
  • If there is a high volume of orders expect delays but we will produce the products as soon as we can.
  • We usually give updates for pending and delayed orders of the customers.
DELIVERY
  • FREE DELIVERY is only covered at Brgy. Marikaban, Santa Fe, Cebu and Brgy. Mojon Boundary. Areas outside the said places will be charged for a delivery fee. Delivery charges
